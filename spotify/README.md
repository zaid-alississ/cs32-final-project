# cs32-final-project
Final project with Khalid and Kim

We will build a model that takes data from Spotify your Spotify account and gives a recommendation for an artist/song that you might like. It assesses whether you are a mainstream listener or a niche listener based on the average monthly listeners of your top artists, and then recommends an artist/song that is either more niche or more mainstream respectively that has similar genres to your top artists' genres.
